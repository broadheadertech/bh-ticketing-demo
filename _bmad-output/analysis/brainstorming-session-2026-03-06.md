## Technique Execution Results

### Technique 1: Role Playing (4 Personas)

#### Persona: The Independent Artist

**[Profiles #1]**: Artist Command Center Dashboard
_Concept_: A unified dashboard showing real-time ticket sales, upcoming events, revenue, and merch orders at a glance. The artist's "mission control" for their entire business.
_Novelty_: Most ticketing platforms show only tickets - this treats the artist as a full business, not just an event host.

**[Profiles #2]**: End-to-End Artist Business Hub
_Concept_: Artists manage tickets, merch, fan communication, and event logistics from a single platform, eliminating the need for multiple tools and manual tracking.
_Novelty_: Moves beyond "ticketing platform" into "artist business platform" territory.

**[Profiles #3]**: Zero-Manual-Entry Booking
_Concept_: Automated guest lists, digital ticket validation, real-time sales tracking - no more spreadsheets, DM confirmations, or cash counting.
_Novelty_: Targets the specific chaos of independent artist event management that big platforms ignore.

**[Content Hub #4]**: Post-Event Recap Dashboard
_Concept_: The morning after, the artist opens the app and sees: total attendance, revenue breakdown (tickets vs merch), fan demographics, top-selling merch items, and a prompt to upload event photos/videos for the Content Hub.
_Novelty_: Turns the post-show high into actionable business intelligence instead of just a memory.

**[Social #5]**: Fan Engagement Afterglow
_Concept_: Fans who attended get a push notification: "Thanks for coming last night! Rate the show, share your photos, and get early access to the next event." Creates a feedback loop that keeps fans engaged between events.
_Novelty_: Most platforms forget the fan after the ticket is scanned. This extends the relationship beyond the event itself.

**[Content Hub #6]**: Auto-Generated Event Highlights
_Concept_: Platform auto-compiles uploaded photos, setlists, and fan reviews into a shareable "Event Recap" page on the artist's portfolio. Fans can tag themselves, share on socials, and relive the experience.
_Novelty_: Artists get free marketing content from their own events without lifting a finger.

**[Analytics #7]**: Post-Event Intelligence Report
_Concept_: Automated report showing which promo channels drove the most sales, peak purchase times, refund rates, and fan retention from previous events. "42% of tonight's audience also attended your March show."
_Novelty_: Gives independent artists the kind of data insights usually reserved for major promoters.

**[Venues #8]**: Smart Venue Matching
_Concept_: When an artist's events consistently sell out, the platform suggests larger venues in their area with availability, pricing, and capacity info.
_Novelty_: Proactively helps artists grow instead of leaving them to figure it out alone.

**[Analytics #9]**: Growth Trajectory Insights
_Concept_: A visual timeline showing the artist's growth - ticket sales per event, audience size trend, revenue curve. Helps artists see their momentum and make confident decisions about scaling up.
_Novelty_: Turns raw data into a growth story that motivates and informs.

**[Collaboration #10]**: Co-Headline Matchmaking
_Concept_: Artist can't fill a 300-seat venue alone yet? Platform suggests other artists with similar audiences to co-headline, splitting the venue cost and combining fanbases.
_Novelty_: Solves the "gap" between small and mid-size venues by enabling collaboration as a scaling strategy.

**[Promotions #11]**: Scaling Promotion Tools
_Concept_: As the artist grows, the platform unlocks more promotion tools - from simple social sharing at 50 fans, to email campaigns at 200, to sponsored event placement at 500.
_Novelty_: Tiered tools prevent overwhelm for small artists while giving power users what they need.

**[Sponsorship #12]**: Micro-Sponsorship Marketplace
_Concept_: Local businesses can sponsor smaller events directly through the platform. A bar sponsors a 200-person show in exchange for logo placement on the ticket and event page.
_Novelty_: Democratizes sponsorship - not just for big festivals, but for growing independent artists too.

#### Persona: The Event Organizer (Organization)

**[Profiles #13]**: Organization Multi-Event Dashboard
_Concept_: Organizations manage dozens of concurrent and upcoming events. Dashboard shows all events in a calendar/list view with status indicators - on sale, sold out, draft, completed - with roll-up revenue and attendance stats.
_Novelty_: Treats organizations as portfolio managers, not single-event hosts.

**[Event Portfolio #14]**: Event Templates & Cloning
_Concept_: Clone last week's event, adjust the date and speaker, and publish in 30 seconds. Racing league has 12 rounds? Create one template, generate all 12 events with venue/date variations.
_Novelty_: Eliminates repetitive setup for recurring or series-based events.

**[Ticket Customization #15]**: Event-Type-Aware Ticket Builder
_Concept_: The ticket builder adapts to the event type. Racing: pit access, grandstand zones, paddock passes. Seminar: standard, VIP with lunch, virtual attendance. Concert: GA, VIP, backstage.
_Novelty_: Builds domain intelligence into the ticketing flow instead of generic tiers.

**[Collaboration #16]**: Staff & Volunteer Management
_Concept_: Assign roles - door staff, registration desk, speakers, marshals. Role-based access: "Maria can scan tickets but can't see revenue."
_Novelty_: Turns the platform from a ticketing tool into an event operations tool.

**[Analytics #17]**: Cross-Event Analytics
_Concept_: Compare performance across event types, venues, seasons, and marketing channels. "Your racing events average 340 attendees with 12% merch attach rate."
_Novelty_: Portfolio-level intelligence that helps organizations decide where to invest.

**[Recurring Events #18]**: Season Pass & Subscription Engine
_Concept_: Racing league sells a season pass for all 12 rounds at a discount. Seminar company offers a monthly subscription for unlimited workshops.
_Novelty_: Goes beyond single-event ticketing into relationship-based access models.

**[Sponsorship #19]**: Multi-Event Sponsorship Packages
_Concept_: Organizations sell sponsorship across their entire event portfolio. "Gold sponsor" gets logo on all 12 racing events. Platform tracks deliverables and generates sponsor reports.
_Novelty_: Makes sponsorship management scalable instead of a manual spreadsheet nightmare.

**[Check-in #20]**: Multi-Gate Check-In System
_Concept_: Support multi-gate scanning where each gate validates the correct ticket tier. Real-time dashboard shows capacity per zone.
_Novelty_: Zone-aware access control that adapts to complex venue layouts.

**[Weather #21]**: Event Contingency Manager
_Concept_: Monitor weather and auto-trigger contingency plans: notify attendees of delays, offer reschedule options, manage partial refunds for cancelled sessions.
_Novelty_: Proactive event management instead of reactive chaos when weather disrupts outdoor events.

#### Persona: The Attendee

**[Event Discovery #22]**: Personalized Event Feed
_Concept_: Based on attendance history, followed artists, and interests, the platform serves a curated "For You" feed. Mix of algorithmic and editorial picks.

_Concept_: Your ticket is a beautifully designed digital collectible. After the event, it updates with the actual setlist, your seat location, event photos.

**[Calendar Sync #28]**: Smart Event Calendar

_Novelty_: Goes beyond basic "add to calendar" into proactive event planning.

**[Notifications #29]**: Price Drop & Availability Alerts
_Concept_: Get notified when early bird pricing is about to expire, when the event is 80% sold, or when a new ticket tier opens up.
_Novelty_: Creates urgency without being spammy.

**[Attendee Profiles #30]**: Event History & Memories Timeline
_Concept_: A visual timeline of every event you've attended. Each entry shows the ticket design, photos, your review, and who you went with.

**[Accessibility #31]**: Accessibility-First Booking Flow
_Concept_: During checkout, flag accessibility needs - wheelchair, hearing loop, companion seat. Platform automatically filters available seats and shows accessibility features per venue.

#### Persona: The Venue Manager

**[Venues #32]**: Venue Profile & Availability Calendar
_Concept_: Venues have their own profile page showcasing capacity, layout options, amenities, photos, and a real-time availability calendar.
_Novelty_: Turns the platform into a two-sided marketplace.

_Concept_: Venues list sponsorship opportunities tied to their physical space - lobby banners, stage branding, naming rights.

### Technique 2: SCAMPER Method

#### SCAMPER on Ticket Sales & Customization

**[Ticket Sales #40]**: Multi-Ticket Purchase Flow
_Concept_: Buy multiple tickets in a single transaction - for friends, family, or groups. Select different seat locations, different ticket tiers, and assign names/emails to each ticket.
_Novelty_: Goes beyond simple quantity selection into per-ticket customization within one checkout.

**[Ticket Sales #41]**: Ticket Gifting
_Concept_: Buy a ticket as a gift for someone else with a beautifully designed digital gift ticket and personal message.
_Novelty_: Turns ticket purchasing into a gifting experience - drives sales from people who aren't even attending.

**[Social #42]**: Group Booking Coordinator
_Concept_: One person initiates a group booking, selects seats together, and sends payment requests to each member.
_Novelty_: Solves the "who's paying" and "can we sit together" chaos in one flow.

**[Ticket Sales #43]**: Bulk Purchase for Organizations
_Concept_: Companies or schools buy tickets in bulk for team outings or field trips. Special bulk pricing, single invoice, distribute individual tickets via email or CSV.
_Novelty_: Opens up B2B ticket sales as a revenue channel.

**[Ticket Sales #44]**: Dynamic Pricing Engine
_Concept_: Like airlines - ticket price adjusts based on demand, time until event, and remaining capacity. Organizers set floor and ceiling prices.
_Novelty_: Maximizes revenue for popular events while making slow events more accessible.

**[Ticket Customization #45]**: Living Tickets
_Concept_: The ticket is a living digital object that updates over time. Pre-event: countdown, parking info. During: schedule changes. Post-event: memory card with photos and stats.
_Novelty_: The ticket becomes a companion throughout the entire event lifecycle.

**[Merch + Tickets #46]**: Ticket-Merch Bundles at Checkout
_Concept_: During ticket purchase: "Add a concert t-shirt for 20% off? Pre-order and pick up at the merch booth - skip the line."
_Novelty_: Captures impulse merch purchases when excitement is highest.

**[Discovery + Social #47]**: Social Discovery Engine
_Concept_: "3 friends are attending this event" on listings. Browse events friends are going to. Create shared wishlists.
_Novelty_: Combines event discovery with social proof.

**[Seat Selection #48]**: Airline-Style Seat Upgrades
_Concept_: After purchasing GA, get offered upgrades closer to the event. "Upgrade to VIP for just 500 more - only 3 left."
_Novelty_: Secondary revenue opportunity from existing ticket holders.

**[Attendee Profiles #49]**: Hotel Loyalty-Style Rewards
_Concept_: Frequent attendees earn points per ticket purchase. Points unlock perks: priority seat selection, free upgrades, early access.
_Novelty_: Drives repeat purchases and platform loyalty.

**[Events #50]**: Micro-Events
_Concept_: Support intimate 10-person wine tastings, 5-person masterclasses, 1-on-1 artist meet-and-greets.
_Novelty_: Opens up a long tail of small, high-value events.

**[Ticket Customization #51]**: Tiered Experience Packages
_Concept_: Sell "experiences" not just "seats." Racing: "Pit Walk + Driver Meet + Grandstand = Ultimate Fan Package."
_Novelty_: Shifts from selling access to selling experiences.

**[Content Hub #52]**: Event Content Marketplace
_Concept_: Post-event recordings, workshop materials, race footage sold as digital content. "Missed the seminar? Buy the recording."
_Novelty_: Events become content assets that keep earning.

**[Profiles #53]**: Artist/Org Booking Agent
_Concept_: Venues or organizers browse artist profiles and send booking requests directly. Platform takes a small commission.
_Novelty_: Flips the platform to also enable "venues hire artists."

**[Ticket Sales #54]**: Zero-Fee for Free Events
_Concept_: Free events listed and managed with zero platform fees. Registration, check-in, and attendee management still work.
_Novelty_: Removes barriers, builds adoption and future paying customers.

**[Check-in #55]**: No-App Check-In
_Concept_: Ticket QR code works from email, screenshot, or Apple/Google Wallet. No app download required.
_Novelty_: Eliminates the #1 attendee complaint.

**[Events #56]**: Attendee-Requested Events
_Concept_: Fans request events. "I want a jazz night in Cebu - who's in?" When enough people pledge, the platform matches an artist and venue.
_Novelty_: Completely reverses the traditional model to demand-driven event creation.

**[Ticket Sales #57]**: Pay-What-You-Want Events
_Concept_: Attendees choose their price within a range. Shows real-time average of what others paid. Social pricing dynamic.
_Novelty_: Democratizes access while letting generous supporters pay more.
